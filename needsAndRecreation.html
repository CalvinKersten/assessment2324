Recreation in Sassenheim

We understand that family time is very important too.

That's why we have listed the top 5 recreational activities:

  1. Wasbeek (swimming pool)
  2. Park Rusthof
  3. Fascinus Jongeren Centrum
  4. Skatepark Sassenheim
  5. Beatrix Playground

Recreation in close proximity to Sassenheim

Personal recommendations for the outgoing families are:

  1. Rent a boat and sail through the canals of Leiden.
  2. Visit the Keukenhof for a beautiful day of flower sightseeing.
  3. Rent a bike and cycle through the amazing flower fields and meadows.

Not every fun activity costs money. You can visit Noordwijk aan Zee or Noordwijkerhout for a nice day at the beach, in the dunes or in the woods.
