Having an infant and still having to get into work isn't always ideal, therefore having a day care close by could help you split your work and home life.

Secondary schools

Secondary schools prepare their students for college and university. Finding a secondary school that suits your child is therefore a must. Sassenheim offers only one secondary school:

  • Het Rijnlands Lyceum (RLS)

Other secondary schools in semi-close proximity are:

  1. Teylingen College-KTS, Voorhout
  2. Het Rijnlands Lyceum (RLO), Oegstgeest
  3. Duinzicht, Oegstgeest
  4. Da Vinci College, Leiden
