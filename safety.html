Sassenheim at daytime

A important factor, if not the most important when moving is the safety of the town/city you are moving to.

This factor plays a humongous roll in your daily life. Can you let your kid go to school themselves? Can you even let them play outside? There is one thing I can assure you. And that's that you won't need to worry about their safety outside. Sassenheim is a small town filled with lots of reasonable people. We're not telling you there isn't a single soul looking to do harm, but that's always be the case, everywhere.

Take a look at Drimble.nl for more details into crimes in Sassenheim.

Sassenheim at nighttime

Believe it or not, being outside when it's dark doesn't make it any more dangerous.

From Monday until Thursday it'll be quiet. Most of the people that are outside at night are walking their dogs. On Friday and Saturday the pubs will have open doors till late at night. Especially the Hoofdstraat, Sassenheim will be a bit busier. If you don"t like having to walk or cycle past groups of people at night, you could always take a short detour, as only the Hoofdstraat will contain more people.

Conclusion

Sassenheim is a safe place to be. Kids can go to school, elderly can do their groceries and dog owners can walk their dogs. And at night it's the exact same story. But no matter what. Always pay just a little more attention when outside alone at night. Safety first.
